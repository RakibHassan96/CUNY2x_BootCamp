Valve Corporation



Overview

Valve Corporation is an American video game developer and digital distribution company founded on August 24, 1996. One of its notable creations is the software distribution platform known as Steam. The company has also developed some of the gaming industry's most notable games such as:

  • Half-Life series
  • Counter-Strike
  • Portal
  • Team Fortress
  • Left 4 Dead series

Founders

Valve was founded by former Microsoft employees Gabe Newell and Mike Harrington.
